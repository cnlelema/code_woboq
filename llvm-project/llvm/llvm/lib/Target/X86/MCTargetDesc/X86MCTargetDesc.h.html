112/// Returns the sub or super register of a specific X86 register.
113/// e.g. getX86SubSuperRegister(X86::EAX, 16) returns X86::AX.
114/// Aborts on error.
115unsigned getX86SubSuperRegister(unsigned, unsigned, bool High=false);
